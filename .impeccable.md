## Design Context

### Users
Ukrainian driving school students (ages 17–35) preparing for PDR exams and obtaining a driver's license. Secondary: driving instructors managing their schedule and students; school managers handling leads and communications. Students use the app on Android mid-range phones, often on the go. Instructors use it between sessions. Context: educational, goal-oriented — users want to learn, book lessons, and communicate with the school quickly.

### Brand Personality
**Professional · Trustworthy · Ukrainian**
— Confidence without arrogance. The tone of a skilled instructor who knows the rules and explains them clearly.
— Serious enough to convey expertise, warm enough to reduce anxiety around driving tests.
— Premium mobile product built in Ukraine, for Ukrainians.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Mobile (dark, OLED)**: Ultra-dark OLED black `#000000` background, deep charcoal cards `#0A0A0A–#121212`, brand red `#E51D1D` as primary accent (buttons, active states, badges), white `#F5F5F5` as text. ScalePressable micro-animations on all tappable elements. Reduced border-radius (6–16px), ultra-soft shadows. The feel: premium Ukrainian fintech/edtech — think Monobank meets a government-grade document flow.

**Web (light, glass)**: Clean white/light-gray surfaces, glassmorphism accents, red as brand color, modern typography, lots of air. Anti-reference: old Ukrainian government sites, garish green-on-dark color schemes. Target feel: modern European edtech landing.

**Admin**: Utilitarian clarity. Fast tables, clear status badges, no decoration for decoration's sake.

### Design Principles
1. **Progressive disclosure** — show the essential, hide the detail behind a tap. No screen should show more than 5–7 primary actions.
2. **Real data, always** — no mock labels, no placeholder subtitles in production UI. If data isn't loaded, show a skeleton or empty state.
3. **Role clarity** — student, instructor, manager each get a coherent UX suited to their job. No role should see a stripped or empty version.
4. **Ukrainian context** — all copy in Ukrainian (interface), professional tone. Error messages are helpful, not technical.
5. **APK is truth** — no feature is done until verified on a real Android build via ADB.
