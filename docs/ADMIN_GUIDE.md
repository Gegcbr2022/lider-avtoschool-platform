# Admin Guide

The CRM is designed around the operational workflow:

1. New lead arrives from site, mobile or Telegram.
2. Manager qualifies the lead and schedules consultation.
3. Contract and documents are attached.
4. Payment intent is created and webhook confirms status.
5. Student is moved into LMS and practice booking.
6. Notifications remind the student about lessons, payments and exams.

Roles:

- Admin: full access, settings, exports, audit logs.
- Manager: leads, clients, bookings, documents and payments.
- Student: personal profile, course progress, practice, documents and notifications.
